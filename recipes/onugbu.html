Onugbu Soup Recipe

Ingredients

  • Bitter leaves (Onugbu)
  • Palm oil
  • Stockfish
  • Goat meat
  • Ground crayfish
  • Seasoning cubes
  • Salt
  • Ground pepper
  • Water

Instructions

  1. Wash the bitter leaves thoroughly to reduce bitterness.
  2. Boil the goat meat and stockfish with seasoning cubes and salt until tender.
  3. Add palm oil to the pot and allow it to mix well with the stock.
  4. Stir in the ground crayfish and pepper.
  5. Add the washed bitter leaves and let it cook for a few minutes.
  6. Adjust seasoning to taste and serve hot.
