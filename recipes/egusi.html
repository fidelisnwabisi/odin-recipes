Egusi Soup Recipe

Ingredients

  • 2 cups of ground egusi (melon seeds)
  • 1/2 cup of palm oil
  • 1 lb of assorted meat (beef, goat, or chicken)
  • 1 smoked fish
  • 1 cup of spinach or bitter leaves
  • 1 medium onion (chopped)
  • 2 tablespoons of ground crayfish
  • 2-3 scotch bonnet peppers (blended)
  • Salt and seasoning cubes to taste
  • 4 cups of water or stock

Instructions

  1. Heat the palm oil in a pot and saut√© the chopped onions until translucent.
  2. Add the blended peppers and cook for 5 minutes.
  3. Stir in the ground egusi and fry for another 5 minutes.
  4. Add the meat, smoked fish, and stock. Simmer for 15 minutes.
  5. Mix in the crayfish, spinach, and seasoning. Cook for another 10 minutes.
  6. Adjust salt and seasoning to taste. Serve hot with pounded yam or rice.
