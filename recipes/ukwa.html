Ukwa Recipe

Home

Ingredients

  • 2 cups African breadfruit (ukwa)
  • 1 teaspoon salt
  • Water (as needed)
  • Optional: Palm oil, crayfish, or seasoning cubes

Instructions

  1. Rinse the ukwa thoroughly to remove any dirt or debris.
  2. Place the ukwa in a pot and add enough water to cover it.
  3. Cook on medium heat until the ukwa becomes soft (about 1-2 hours).
  4. Add salt and any optional ingredients like palm oil or crayfish.
  5. Simmer for an additional 10 minutes and serve warm.
