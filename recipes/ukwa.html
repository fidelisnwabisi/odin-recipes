Ukwa Recipe

Description

Ukwa soup, also known as African breadfruit soup, is a traditional Igbo delicacy in Nigeria, made from the cooking liquid or broth of boiled African breadfruit (Ukwa). It's a flavorful and nutritious soup, often enjoyed alongside the breadfruit porridge itself. The soup is known for its rich aroma and is considered a special treat within Igbo culture.

Steps

  • 2 cups African breadfruit (ukwa)
  • 1 teaspoon salt
  • Water (as needed)
  • Optional: Palm oil, crayfish, or seasoning cubes

Instructions

  1. Rinse the ukwa thoroughly to remove any dirt or debris.
  2. Place the ukwa in a pot and add enough water to cover it.
  3. Cook on medium heat until the ukwa becomes soft (about 1-2 hours).
  4. Add salt and any optional ingredients like palm oil or crayfish.
  5. Simmer for an additional 10 minutes and serve warm.
