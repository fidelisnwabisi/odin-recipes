Fio-Fio Recipe

Ingredients

  • 1 cup of fio-fio beans
  • 2 tablespoons of palm oil
  • 1 medium onion, chopped
  • 1 teaspoon of ground crayfish
  • Salt to taste
  • Optional: pepper or other spices

Instructions

  1. Soak the fio-fio beans overnight and rinse thoroughly.
  2. Boil the beans until tender, then drain excess water.
  3. Heat palm oil in a pan and sauté the onions until golden.
  4. Add the cooked beans, ground crayfish, and spices to the pan.
  5. Stir well and cook for an additional 5-10 minutes.
  6. Serve hot and enjoy!
